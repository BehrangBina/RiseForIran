The Four Pillars

The foundation of our vision for Iran's future

National Unity

Bringing together all Iranians regardless of ethnicity, religion, or background. Building a nation where diversity is our strength and unity is our foundation for a prosperous future.

Secularism

Establishing a government separate from religion, where freedom of belief is protected and no citizen is discriminated against based on their faith or lack thereof.

Democracy

Creating a truly democratic system where the people's voice determines their future through free and fair elections, with a government accountable to its citizens.

Human Rights

Guaranteeing fundamental human rights for all, including freedom of speech, women's rights, minority rights, and the dignity of every individual under the rule of law.

About Us

We are a nonprofit, community based Iranian Australian organisation in Melbourne, united by a shared commitment to uplift, support, and empower people of Iranian heritage and the broader Australian community. Guided by the timeless Persian values of human dignity, fairness, and mutual respect, and proudly embracing Australia's core principles of the fair go, equality before the law, freedom of speech, and the right of every individual to live in safety, we work to build a stronger, more inclusive future while honouring our rich heritage.

As members of Australian society, we are deeply committed to the safety and security of our country. We recognise the real danger posed by the Islamic Republic of Iran and its operatives inside Australia and around the world. These networks threaten the peace, freedom, and multicultural harmony that make Australia the safe and welcoming nation we all cherish.

That is why we actively stand against the influence of the Islamic Republic and its operatives on Australian soil. By raising awareness, fostering unity across communities, and working collaboratively with like minded groups, we strive to see these dangerous networks removed, not only from Australia but from free societies worldwide. A world free of the Islamic Republic's operatives is a safer Australia and a safer world.

Our Activities

  • Organising cultural and educational events, Persian festival stalls, and community gatherings that celebrate our heritage while promoting unity, integration, and cultural awareness among all Australians.
  • Running information booths in collaboration with the global 100 Cities for Iran initiative at 100cities.org to amplify the message of His Imperial Highness Crown Prince Reza Pahlavi and to combat antisemitism and all forms of hate in Australia.
  • Partnering with other Iranian Australian and allied organisations on advocacy campaigns that highlight human rights, women's rights, and the need for democratic change in Iran.
  • Supporting the recent designation of the Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC) as a state sponsor of terrorism in Australia, and advocating for continued enforcement against IRGC activities, consistent with actions already taken by the United States, Canada, and other democratic nations.

We proudly and unequivocally support His Imperial Highness Crown Prince Reza Pahlavi as the legitimate opposition leader against the Islamic Republic regime. We believe his vision reflects the true aspirations of the Iranian people for freedom, secular democracy, human rights, human dignity, and the restoration of Iran's national sovereignty and territorial integrity. We adopt his principles and code of conduct as the moral and practical framework for everything we do.

By living these values here in Melbourne and across Australia, we strengthen our local communities while contributing to the global movement for a free, democratic, and secular Iran.

Together, we rise for liberty, dignity, safety, and hope for Iran, for Australia, and for the world.

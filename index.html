Welcome to Rise For Iran

Rise For Iran is a movement dedicated to honoring the rich history of Iran while advocating for a brighter future. We believe in the power of unity, resilience, and hope to bring about positive change in our society.

Join us as we celebrate our heritage, raise awareness about current issues, and work together to create a better tomorrow for all Iranians.

Purpose

The purposes of the association are to uphold and promote Australian democratic values, including respect for the law, freedom of expression, fairness, multiculturalism, and peaceful civic participation, and to strengthen solidarity within the Iranian-Australian community in a manner aligned with these values. The association operates in accordance with Australian laws and democratic institutions and seeks to support civic understanding, cultural integration and responsible participation among Iranian-Australians, contributing to social cohesion and positive outcomes for Australian society. Through lawful and non-violent advocacy, the association encourages informed public discussion and facilitates constructive dialogue with politicians and policymakers, civil society organisations and democratic institutions in Australia. Within this Australian-focused framework, the association also supports democratic aspirations and contributes constructively to global efforts aimed at achieving a free, democratic and accountable future for Iran, while ensuring that the primary focus and benefit of its activities remain within Australia.

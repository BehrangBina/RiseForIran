Welcome to Rise For Iran

Rise for Iran is a Melbourne-based incorporated association of Iranian-Australians and supporters who advocate for freedom, democracy, human rights and the rule of law in Iran. We work to amplify the voices of people inside Iran who are resisting authoritarian rule and to raise awareness in Australia, across Oceania and internationally. Through community engagement, public education and lawful advocacy, we promote democratic values, civic participation and international solidarity in support of a free and democratic future for Iran. We believe in the power of unity, resilience, and hope to bring about positive change in our society.

Join us as we celebrate our heritage, raise awareness about current issues, and work together to create a better tomorrow for all Iranians.

Purpose

The purposes of the association are to uphold and promote Australian democratic values, including respect for the law, freedom of expression, fairness, multiculturalism, and peaceful civic participation, and to strengthen solidarity within the Iranian-Australian community in a manner aligned with these values. The association operates in accordance with Australian laws and democratic institutions and seeks to support civic understanding, cultural integration and responsible participation among Iranian-Australians, contributing to social cohesion and positive outcomes for Australian society. Through lawful and non-violent advocacy, the association encourages informed public discussion and facilitates constructive dialogue with politicians and policymakers, civil society organisations and democratic institutions in Australia. Within this Australian-focused framework, the association also supports democratic aspirations and contributes constructively to global efforts aimed at achieving a free, democratic and accountable future for Iran, while ensuring that the primary focus and benefit of its activities remain within Australia.

Explicit Alignment with Australian Civic and Democratic Values

Rise for Iran operates in accordance with Australia's democratic system, the rule of law and the principles of peaceful civic engagement. The Association supports freedom of expression, political participation, respect for diversity and multiculturalism, and constructive engagement with elected representatives and public institutions. All activities are conducted lawfully, non-violently and transparently, reflecting Australiaâ€™s commitment to democracy, human rights and an open, inclusive society.

Mission

To support freedom, democracy and human rights in Iran by amplifying the voices of the Iranian people, engaging the Iranian-Australian community in peaceful civic action, and promoting informed dialogue within Australia and the international community.

Vision

A free, democratic and accountable Iran where human rights are respected, citizens participate openly in civic life, and governance is grounded in the rule of law.


Core Activities

Cultural & Community Engagement

We organise cultural and educational events, Persian festival stalls, and community gatherings that celebrate our heritage while fostering unity and cultural awareness across Australia.

Welfare & Support Services

We provide welfare support for migrants, youth, and seniors, alongside educational programs and partnerships with nonprofits.

Advocacy & Public Awareness

Running information booths in collaboration with the global 100 Cities for Iran initiative at 100cities.org to amplify the message of His Imperial Highness Crown Prince Reza Pahlavi and to combat antisemitism and all forms of hate in Australia.

Human Rights & Democratic Change

Partnering with other Iranian Australian and allied organisations on advocacy campaigns that highlight human rights, women's rights, and the need for democratic change in Iran.

We proudly and unequivocally support His Imperial Highness Crown Prince Reza Pahlavi as the legitimate opposition leader against the Islamic Republic regime. We believe his vision reflects the true aspirations of the Iranian people for freedom, secular democracy, human rights, human dignity, and the restoration of Iran's national sovereignty and territorial integrity. We adopt his principles and code of conduct as the moral and practical framework for everything we do. By living these values here in Melbourne and across Australia, we strengthen our local communities while contributing to the global movement for a free, democratic, and secular Iran.
Together, we rise for liberty, dignity, safety, and hope for Iran, for Australia, and for the world.

100 Cities Project & Participating Cities Worldwide

To the Jewish and Israeli Communities in Australia and Around the World,

On behalf of the 100 Cities Project and its network of participating cities across the globe, we unequivocally condemn the heinous terror attack near Bondi Beach during Hanukkah, which targeted innocent civilians in a time of peace and celebration.

We stand in solidarity with the Jewish community, with all victims and families affected, and with all those who cherish human dignity, security, and peace. Terror in any form; whether driven by hatred, prejudice, or political extremism; has no place in our societies.

As Iranians and supporters of freedom who have lived under extremism, repression, and state-sponsored hatred, we hold deep respect for the Jewish and Israeli values of life, dignity, remembrance, and perseverance. Targeting innocent people because of their faith or identity is an attack on our shared humanity.

Our movement was founded on the belief that human rights, democracy, and respect for all peoples are fundamental to a free and just world. We reject violence against civilians and honor the memory of all those harmed on a sacred night of light, hope, and unity.

We extend our deepest condolences to the families and communities touched by this tragedy. We reaffirm our commitment to peaceful coexistence and mutual respect among all cultures, religions, and nations.

We believe that in a New, free Iran, we will soon be able to rebuild and strengthen the deep-rooted friendship and historic relationship between the Iranian and Jewish peoples, and with Israel; contributing to a free, peaceful Middle East, guided by shared principles of justice, dignity, and mutual respect, inspired by the spirit of the Cyrus Accord.

May this sorrow remind us of the shared values that bind humanity: dignity, compassion, and the unyielding resolve to protect the innocent.
